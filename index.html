Data Analytics Journey

Having graduated with a bachelor's degree in computer science, programming and data management were always part of my core skills. I stepped into the field of data analytics in 2016, when I started my professional career with Mu Sigma Inc., a pure-play data analytics consultancy based out of Bangalore, India. At Mu Sigma, I was fortunate to work for six Fortune 500 companies: Adidas, Pfizer, Footlocker, Kellogs, Nike, and Johnson & Johnson. During my four-year tenure at Mu Sigma, I held the positions of Trainee Decision Scientist, Decision Scientist, and Apprentice Lead. 

In October 2020, I was employed by Jones Lang LaSalle as an Assistant Manager for data analytics. At JLL, my primary responsibility was to support the marketing vertical. I worked alongside marketing heads and their teams to analyze and generate reports and insights on the performances and finances of the marketing efforts.

In September 2022, I moved to Sudbury, Canada, to pursue a Post-Graduate Certification in Business Analytics from Cambrian College of Arts and Technology. At Cambrian, I had the opportunity to expand my analytics knowledge under the guidance of exceptional professors and collaborate with brilliant individuals from across the globe.
